Privacy Policy for BrawnyZip

Last updated: June 2026

BrawnyZip ("the App") is a puzzle game developed by Rohit Kori. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

BrawnyZip does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The App does not require account creation, login, or internet connectivity to play.

Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only, using standard device storage:

  • Your current level progress
  • Your total stars earned

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

BrawnyZip does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

BrawnyZip does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
